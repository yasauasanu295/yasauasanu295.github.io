Triple 180gm vinyl LP pressing. This 80"s 12 Inch Remixes Collected edition is from the decade when the extended remix was an artform. A remix is usually an alternative, and sometimes longer, version of a previous recorded song. The 80"s saw a boom in technology, meaning better equipment and possibilities to rearrange a song completely which gave DJs a change to play a version of a well-known track to their dancefloors. This 3LP Collected compilation brings together some of the popular versions from that influential era and includes 12 inch remixes by Queen, The Cure, Simple Minds, Culture Club and 17 more tracks.
