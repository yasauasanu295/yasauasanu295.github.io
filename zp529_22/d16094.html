THE INGREDIENTS
An invigorating fusion of juicy Italian bergamot and zesty bloody orange opens the fragrance, with red ginger adding a warm spiciness to the citrus top notes. The vibrant energy of these sparkling citruses evolves to a powerfully masculine heart of aromatic clary sage and crisp Egyptian geranium essence, enlivened by spicy notes of cardamom essence and cardamom absolute.Mysterious patchouli gives the fragrance an intensely alluring base, its richness and subtle smokiness balanced by earthy Haitian vetiver and woody ambery notes.
