PRIMAVERA IN ANTICIPO is the ninth studio album by Laura Pausini originally released in 2008, reissued on 180-gram Tiffany Green vinyl. Includes the bonus tracks "Primavera in Anticipo (live Italian/Spanish)" and "Invece No (live Italian/Spanish)", which the artist presented live on 14 November 2008 in Piazza di Spagna (Rome). 14 total tracks on 3 sides including "Primavera in Anticipo (It Is My Song)", presented both in solo version and in duet with the English singer-songwriter James Blunt. Pausini is the principle songwriter on the album except for "Prima Che Esci" by Gianluca Grignani and "Primavera in Anticipo (It Is My Song)" by James Blunt. Laura won the Wind Music Award Italy in the summer of 2009 and the Latin Grammy Award for Best Female Pop Album in the same year.(ラウラパウジーニ)
